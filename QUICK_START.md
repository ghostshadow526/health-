# health+ Quick Start Guide

## 🚀 Get Started in 3 Easy Steps

### Step 1: Run the Application

**Using Python (Recommended)**
```bash
python -m http.server 8000
```

**Using Node.js**
```bash
npx http-server -p 8000
```

Then open your browser and go to: `http://localhost:8000`

### Step 2: Create Your Account

1. Click **"Sign Up"** on the homepage
2. Fill in your information:
   - Name, age, and sex
   - Email and password
   - Select your medical condition(s)
3. Click **"Create Account"**

### Step 3: Start Tracking

You're now in the dashboard! You can:
- **Add Data**: Record your vitals (blood pressure, heart rate, etc.)
- **View Charts**: See your health trends over time
- **Set Reminders**: Never forget medications or appointments
- **Learn**: Read health tips specific to your condition

## 📋 Important Features

### Recording Health Data
- Go to **"Add Data"** tab
- Enter your measurements
- Click **"Save Health Data"**

### Creating Reminders
- Go to **"Reminders"** tab
- Click **"+ Add Reminder"**
- Set type, title, and time
- Click **"Save Reminder"**

### Viewing Your Progress
- The **"Overview"** tab shows:
  - Your latest vitals at a glance
  - Interactive charts showing trends
  - Color-coded metrics

## 🔐 Login Credentials

After registration, use your email and password to log in anytime at:
`http://localhost:8000/login.html`

## 💡 Tips for Best Results

1. **Be Consistent**: Record your vitals at the same time each day
2. **Add Notes**: Include context like "after exercise" or "before medication"
3. **Set Reminders**: Use the reminder feature to build healthy habits
4. **Review Trends**: Check your charts weekly to spot patterns
5. **Read Tips**: Visit the Health Tips tab regularly for guidance

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

**Problem: Can't log in**
- Check your email and password are correct
- Make sure you've already registered

**Problem: Data not saving**
- Ensure you have internet connection
- Check that you filled in at least one health metric

**Problem: Charts not showing**
- You need to add at least one health data entry first
- Try refreshing the page

## 📱 Using on Mobile

The app works great on mobile devices:
1. Open the app URL on your phone's browser
2. Add to home screen for quick access:
   - **iOS**: Tap Share → Add to Home Screen
   - **Android**: Tap Menu → Add to Home Screen
